Migrating Between Services

AniList → MAL

Enter your AniList username to download a MAL-compatible XML file, then import it at myanimelist.net/import.php.

⚠ Anime with no MAL equivalent will be skipped.

MAL → AniList

AniList natively accepts MAL's export format — no conversion needed. Follow these steps:

  1. 1On MAL, go to Profile → Export and download your anime list XML.
  2. 2On AniList, go to Settings → Import.
  3. 3Select MyAnimeList as the source and upload the XML file.
  4. 4AniList will match entries and import your statuses, scores, and progress.
